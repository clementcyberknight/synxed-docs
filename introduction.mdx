Synxed is a JavaScript and TypeScript SDK that gives your website, game, or app access to licensed music playback without the legal complexity. Whether you need a simple floating player or a fully custom audio experience, Synxed provides the building blocks to get music playing in minutes — backed by a reliable, browser-native API that works across every major frontend framework.

## What You Can Play

Synxed supports a wide range of audio content types out of the box:

- **Songs** — play individual tracks by song ID
- **Playlists** — stream curated or user-defined playlists by playlist code
- **Mixes** — play programmatic or editorial mixes by mix ID
- **Live Radio** — tune into continuous live radio streams
- **Scheduled Stations** — play time-aware stations that follow a broadcast schedule

## Key Capabilities

Beyond basic playback, Synxed ships with a full feature set for production apps:

- **Voice commands** — let users control playback hands-free
- **Resumable playback** — pick up exactly where a listener left off
- **Ads and ad skip state** — built-in ad support with skip tracking
- **Ready-made web player UI** — a polished floating player you can drop in with one call
- **Low-level player API** — full programmatic control for building your own custom interface

## Framework Support

The SDK targets browser JavaScript and TypeScript. It works with all major environments:

- React and Next.js
- Vue
- Angular
- Plain HTML pages
- Browser-based games and app shells
